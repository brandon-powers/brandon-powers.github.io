About Me

Hi, I'm Brandon Powers! I'm a junior studying Computer Science at Stonehill College. I have a ton of goals after I graduate, and a very clear career choice: I want to become a software engineer. The goal is to build something truly awesome, then do it all over again! I hope to be in the Boston, MA area and work for a company like Google, Facebook, Amazon, and the like. Most importantly, I want to contribute and be a part of a company that does great things. So why Computer Science?

Computer Science is, in my opinion, truly expressive. That was the reason I started this journey of getting my B.S. in CS (no pun intended). Think of any other field of study where you can literally create something one day that can affect millions of people the next. There aren't many, but Computer Science can do just that. Are you viewing this website on a laptop, a mobile phone, a tablet? Hardware engineers created the CPU running that device; software engineers programmed the operating system allowing you to use that hardware, and different software engineers created applications and a user interface to make that operating system accessible to a user. So when I think of why I study Computer Science, I can't help but be amazed at this opportunity to work on what is quite honestly the most ubiquitous invention ever made: computers.
